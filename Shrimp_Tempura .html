Shrimp Tempura

Ingredients Directions

jumbo or medium shrimp


all-purpose flour


cornstarch


egg


water (icy-cold)











 Prepare Shrimp

Step 1 Prepare the shrimp, by deveining them and removing the shell right up to the tail.


Step 2 Next, make 4 to 5 small notches on the “belly” of each shrimp. This will prevent them from curling up during frying.


Step 3 Toss shrimp in cornstarch


Make The Batter

Step 1 Mix cold egg with water in a bowl. Add flour and stir in. Do not overmix.


Step 2 Dip each shrimp in batter.


Fry

Step 1 Heat up oil to 340 to 360 degrees F.


Step 2 Drop 3 to 4 batter coated shrimp in the pot and fry for 2 to 3 minutes per side.


Step 3 Remove shrimp onto a paper towel-lined plate.
