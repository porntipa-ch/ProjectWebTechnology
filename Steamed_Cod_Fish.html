Steamed Cod Fish

Ingredients Directions

a serving of cod fillet


a touch of salt


2 tablespoons cooking wine


a handful of thinly sliced ginger strips


1 small hot chili pepper, sliced (optional)


a handful of shredded scallion


1 tablespoon seasoned soy sauce for seafood


2 tablespoons oil

Step 1 Marinate the cod fillet with salt and cooking wine for 10 minutes.


Step 2 Top the cod fillet with ginger and chili pepper.


Step 3 After the water starts to boil, continue to steam for 5 minutes.


Step 4 Remove from steamer, dress with seasoned soy sauce for seafood, and place the scallion to top.


Step 5 Heat oil in a cast iron pan until reaching smoke point, carefully pour the hot oil onto the fish.


Step 6 Serve and enjoy while hot!
