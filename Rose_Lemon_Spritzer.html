Rose Lemon Spritzer

Ingredients Directions

2 tablespoons rose water * optional


2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice


1 1/2-2 ounces vodka omit to make a mocktail!


1-2 tablespoon honey or to taste (use agave if vegan)


a few drops of blood orange or pomegranate juice for color (optional)


3/4 cup sparkling water or more to taste


fresh roses for garnish (optional)

Step 1 1. Combine all the rose water, fresh lemon juice, vodka, honey and blood orange or pomegranate juice (if using) in a cocktail shaker and fill with ice. Shake until combined and then strain into a glass. Pour in the sparkling water. Garnish with fresh roses. DRINK!
