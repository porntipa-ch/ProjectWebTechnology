Grapefruit Thyme Mocktail

Ingredients Directions

1 cup granulated sugar


1 cup water


1/2 cup fresh thyme


31/3 cups cold sparkling water


1/2 cup thyme syrup, recipe above


11/3 cups fresh grapefruit juice


2 cups ice cubes


 TO MAKE THE THYME SYRUP

Step 1 Add sugar and water to a pot and bring to the boil. Switch back to a low heat and simmer for about 5 minutes until the sugar has dissolved. Remove from the heat.


Step 2 Add the thyme and cover with the syrup. Allow to steep for 2-3 hours at room temperature.


Step 3 Remove the thyme. Pour the syrup into a glass with a lid and refrigerate. Use within 1-2 weeks.


TO MAKE THE GRAPEFRUIT THYME MOCKTAIL

Step 1 Fill ice cubes into glasses. Add 2 tablespoons thyme syrup to each glass and fill up with 1/3 cup grapefruit juice and approximately 3/4 cup sparkling water. Garnish with a slice of grapefruit and some thyme. Enjoy ice cold.
