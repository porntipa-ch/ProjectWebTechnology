Ten Minute Naan Pizza

Ingredients Directions

4 naan bread pieces


1/2 cup pizza sauce


1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded/sliced


2 Tbsp fresh basil, chopped

Step 1 Preheat oven to 425F.


Step 2 Spread pizza sauce evenly over naan bread. Then sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese - you can also add parmesan cheese if you have on hand.


Step 3 Bake on the oven rack for 8 minutes - the crust and cheese should be golden brown.


Step 4 Slice and serve with fresh basil, parmesan cheese, and any other additional toppings.
