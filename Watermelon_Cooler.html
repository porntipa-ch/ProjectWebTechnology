Watermelon Cooler

Ingredients Directions

550g/1lb 4oz watermelon, peeled, seeds removed, chopped


Â¼ lemon, juice only


fresh mint leaves, to serve

Step 1 Mash the watermelon in a bowl, then stir in the lemon juice.


Step 2 Sieve the watermelon into a jug and press down gently on the pulp to extract all the juice.


Step 3 Pour the juice into glasses filled with ice and garnish with the mint.
