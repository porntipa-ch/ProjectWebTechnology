Sesame Chicken

Ingredients Directions
Sesame Chicken Sauce Ingredients

1/4 cup soy sauce


1/4 cup rice vinegar


1/2 cup brown sugar


1 tablespoon corn starch


1/3 cup water


2 teaspoons sesame oil


1/2 tablespoon sesame seeds


Tempura Batter

1/2 cup cornstarch


1/2 cup flour


2 egg whites


1/2 cup cold seltzer water


Chicken

1 lb chicken thighs boneless and skinless cut into 1? chunks


2 teaspoons sesame oil


1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper


1 tablespoon minced garlic


vegetable oil for deep frying

Step 1 To make the sauce mix all the ingredients together and to prepare the batter, add the ingredients together until just combined.


Step 2 Add the chicken pieces to the batter and coat while heating 3 inches of oil to 350 degrees and add the chicken to the oil in batches frying until golden brown.


Step 3 In a large wok or skillet add the sesame oil to the frying pan and add the garlic and red pepper flakes and cook for 30 seconds.


Step 4 Add in the sauce and the chicken and coat quickly and serve immediately, garnished with sesame seeds.
