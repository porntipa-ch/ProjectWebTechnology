Ham and Cheese Egg Muffins

Ingredients Directions

10 Large Eggs


1/3 Cup Milk


Salt & Pepper to taste


1/2 Teaspoon Garlic Powder


1/4 Teaspoon Onion Powder


1 Teaspoon Hot Sauce, Optional


1 Cup Chopped Broccoli


1 Cup Shredded Cheddar


1 Cup Diced Ham

Step 1 Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray a 12 cup muffin tin with non-stick cooking spray, set aside.


Step 2 In a large bowl whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and hot sauce


Step 3 Divide the egg mixture evenly into the muffin cups.


Step 4 Top each cup evenly with the chopped broccoli, cheddar, and ham.


Step 5 Bake for 20-25 minutes until set.
