Nachos Fried Chicken

Ingredients Directions

7 ounces Doritos


1 chicken breast


Salt


1 cup flour


1 tablespoon spicy paprika


1 tablespoon onion powder


2 eggs


Frying oil

Step 1 Add the Doritos to a blender, and blend until reduced to crumbs.


Step 2 Cut the chicken into smaller pieces and season with salt.


Step 3 In a bowl, mix the flour, paprika and onion powder.


Step 4 Beat the eggs in another bowl and reserve.


Step 5 Coat the chicken with the flour, then with the eggs and finally with the Doritos crumbs.


Step 6 Fry in hot oil until golden and crispy, then drain on paper towels. Enjoy!
