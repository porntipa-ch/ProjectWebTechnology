Sparkling Pineapple Mojito Mocktail

Ingredients Directions

1-2 teaspoons agave


5-6 mint leaves


1 ounce light rum


3 ounces pineapple juice


ice


Voveti Prosecco to fill


slice of fresh pineapple


extra mint leaf sprigs

Step 1 Place agave, mint leaves, and rum into a glass. Using a muddler or a wooden spoon, tamp down on the leaves to break them up a bit and release their flavor.


Step 2 Pour the pineapple juice over the mint rum mixture. Stir.


Step 3 Add ice to fill the glass.


Step 4 Pour Voveti Prosecco over the ice. Repeat as necessary, as bubbles go down, to fill the glass.


Step 5 Enjoy and repeat!
