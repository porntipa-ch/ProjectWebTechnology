Honey-Lime Tilapia and Corn Foil Pack

Ingredients Directions

4 fillets tilapia


2 tbsp. honey


4 limes, thinly sliced


2 ears corn, shucked


2 tbsp. fresh cilantro leaves


kosher salt


Freshly ground black pepper

Step 1 Heat grill to high. Cut 4 sheets of foil about 12" long.


Step 2 Top each piece of foil with a piece of tilapia. Brush tilapia with honey and top with lime, corn and cilantro. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.


Step 3 Grill until tilapia is cooked through and corn tender, about 15 minutes.
