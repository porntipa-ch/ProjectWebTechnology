Dump and Bake Chicken Parmesan Casserole

Ingredients Directions

1 (12 ounce) package whole wheat penne pasta


1 (24 ounce) jar pasta sauce


2â€“3 cups water


1 pound of chicken (cut into small pieces)


2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese


1/2 cup Parmesan cheese


1 cup bread crumbs


salt and pepper to taste

Step 1 Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Spray 9 x 13 baking dish with cooking spray.


Step 2 Dump uncooked noodles, pasta sauce, water, chicken and 1/2 cup mozzarella in baking dish. Add salt and pepper. Mix ingredients together. Make sure liquid covers pasta and chicken.


Step 3 Cover dish with aluminum foil and bake for 20 minutes.


Step 4 Uncover and bake for an additional 10 minutes or until chicken is cooked all the way through.


Step 5 Spread breadcrumbs, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese over pasta dish. Bake uncovered for 10 minutes or until cheese is melted and bread crumbs are golden brown.
