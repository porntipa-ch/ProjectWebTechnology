Cheddar Bacon Ranch Corn

Ingredients Directions

1/2 c. (1 stick) Butter, softened


1 packet ranch seasoning


Freshly ground black pepper


10 ears corn


2 c. shredded Cheddar


6 slices cooked bacon, finely crumbled


freshly chopped chives


Ranch, for drizzling

Step 1 Heat grill to medium high. In a small bowl, stir together butter and ranch seasoning and season with pepper. Rub ranch butter on corn.


Step 2 Grill until lightly charred, 10 minutes. Top with cheddar and cover grill to melt, 2 minutes.


Step 3 Top with cooked bacon and chives and drizzle with ranch.
